Who We Are

WhatIsMyTimeZone.info was founded with a simple yet essential goal: to eliminate the confusion caused by global time differences. In an era of remote work, international travel, and 24/7 digital connectivity, knowing the exact time and offset is no longer a luxury—it's a necessity.

Our Technology

Unlike many tools that rely on imprecise IP-based geolocation, our platform utilizes high-performance browser-level detection and the IANA Time Zone Database. This ensures that the data you see is pulled directly from your device's operating system, providing a "ground truth" that remains accurate even if you are using a VPN or traveling across borders.

What We Provide:

  • ✅ Instant UTC/GMT Offset Detection
  • ✅ Real-time World Clock Synchronization
  • ✅ Detailed IANA Time Zone Identifiers
  • ✅ Technical Device Audits (Resolution & DPI)

Privacy First

We value your privacy. WhatIsMyTimeZone.info does not track your GPS location, store your personal data, or use invasive cookies. Our tools are designed to be lightweight, fast, and secure, serving the global community of developers, travelers, and professionals with integrity.
