The Iconic 3-Hour Time Gap

The primary difference between EST and PST is exactly three hours. Eastern Standard Time (EST) is 5 hours behind UTC (UTC-5), while Pacific Standard Time (PST) is 8 hours behind UTC (UTC-8).

This means that when it is 12:00 PM (noon) in New York (EST), it is only 9:00 AM in Los Angeles (PST). This gap is a constant factor for national television broadcasts, sports events, and business meetings across the United States and Canada.

Standard vs Daylight Time

It is important to note that the names change during the summer. EST becomes EDT (Eastern Daylight Time) and PST becomes PDT (Pacific Daylight Time). However, because both regions usually switch to Daylight Saving at the same time, the 3-hour difference remains consistent throughout the year.
