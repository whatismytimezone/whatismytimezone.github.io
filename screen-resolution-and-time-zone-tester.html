The Ultimate Device Testing Tool

A screen resolution and time zone tester is an essential utility for web developers, digital marketers, and QA engineers. When building responsive websites, knowing the exact viewport dimensions is just as important as ensuring that event timestamps are displayed in the user's correct local time zone.

Our integrated tool provides both data points instantly. Instead of using two different websites, you can audit your device's display capabilities and temporal settings in one glance, making it easier to debug layout shifts or synchronization issues.

More Than Just Pixels

Resolution testing isn't just about width and height. Modern high-DPI displays (like Retina screens) use a device pixel ratio that can affect how images look. Our tester reads these hardware specifications directly from your browser's window object.

By combining this with our IANA time zone detection, WhatIsMyTimeZone.info acts as a comprehensive fingerprinting check. Use this page to verify that your browser is correctly reporting its environmental data, ensuring a smooth and consistent experience across all your favorite web platforms.
